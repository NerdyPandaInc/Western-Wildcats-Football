About the WildCats

Official Website: westernwildcats.org


Western Youth Football Association
Founded in 2001, we offer football and cheerleading programs that emphasize fun, growth, and community for young athletes. Safety is our top priority—our coaches are USA Football–certified and trained in the latest tackle safety techniques to ensure a secure, supportive environment.

USA Football sets the national standard by equipping coaches with cutting-edge resources and certification programs to protect and empower every player.

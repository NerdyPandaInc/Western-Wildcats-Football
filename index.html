About the Wildcats

Western Youth Football Association Inc. is a football and cheerleading association that was founded in 2001. We are dedicated to creating a positive and enjoyable learning experience for young athletes in our community. Safety is our Top Priority. Our coaches undergo extensive safety training and are certified by USA Football in the latest safety techniques, ensuring a safe and supportive environment for all players. USA Football sets the standard for youth football by providing resources, certification, and programs for coaches to learn the latest principles of Tackle Football Safety.

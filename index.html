About the Wildcats

Official Website of the Western WildCats: Official Wildcats Website

Western Youth Football Association: Founded in 2001, we provide football and cheerleading programs focused on fun, growth, and community for young athletes. Safety is our top priority—our coaches are USA Football–certified and trained in the latest tackle safety techniques to ensure a secure, supportive environment. USA Football sets the national standard, equipping coaches with cutting-edge resources and certification programs to protect and empower every player
